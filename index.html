About me

My name is Przemysław and I am from Poland.

My Coding journey

My coding journey began at the age of 14 when I discovered programming through C++. After learning the basics, I became intrigued by web development, which led me to explore HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. However, I quickly realized that front-end development wasn’t my passion. I shifted my focus to back-end development, diving into PHP and SQL. During this time, I worked on several small projects, gaining experience with databases and the fundamentals of server-side programming.

In high school, I began learning Python as part of my preparation for the Matura exams, and I instantly fell in love with its simplicity and elegance. Python’s versatility allowed me to create complex applications with ease. I later discovered its potential for back-end development and started experimenting with the popular Django framework, which opened up exciting new possibilities for me.

To further expand my skills, I learned Java, which helped me secure my first internship at OBR CTM. This opportunity laid the foundation for my professional career. Today, I’m proud to be a full-time back-end developer at OBR CTM, working primarily with Python alongside a variety of other tools and technologies.
